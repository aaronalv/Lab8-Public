VVC Facts

  • Located in Victorville, California
  • Part of the California Community College System
  • Closest colleges are Chaffey College, San Bernandino Valley College, and Barstow Community College
  • District includes Victorville, Hesperia, Apple Valley, Phelan, and Adelanto
  • Created by public vote in 1960
  • First classes were held in 1961 at Victor Valley High School
